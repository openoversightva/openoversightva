Resources

Public Virginia policing datasets

  • Our data (download by department)
  • Our DocumentCloud files
  • Virginia Community Policing Act data, statewide traffic stops 2020-present (~1GB)
    • Now including partial location data!
  • VA Beyond 2020 - statewide incident-based reporting collected by Virginia State Police, submitted to FBI NIBRS
  • Richmond City:
    • Active Calls, Aug 2023-present (~80 MB)

Affiliated regional police oversight databases

  • BPDWatch - Baltimore Police Database
  • Civic Police Data Project - Chicago police misconduct database
  • OpenOversight - The original OpenOversight project run by Lucy Parsons Labs. Started with Chicago, now includes NYC and LA
  • SPD Watch - OpenOversight Seattle run by Seattle Tech Bloc
  • LLEAD - Louisiana police misconduct database
  • CopDB - Community sourced police database - Utah
  • 50-a.org - NYPD police misconduct database
  • Cop the Data - Texas police misconduct/suspension database

National U.S. policing datasets

  • National Police Index - collects POST (police officer standards and training) certification and employment history across the United States
  • Police Data Access Point - national police data catalog / clearinghouse
  • FBI Uniform Crime Reporting - unified statistics reported by local police agencies
  • Stanford Open Policing Project - national traffic stop database

National policing datasets

  • National Police Index - collects police employment history across the United States
